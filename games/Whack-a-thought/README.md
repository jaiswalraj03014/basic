# 🎯 Whack-a-Thought - Cognitive Behavioral Therapy Game

## Overview
Whack-a-Thought is a therapeutic cognitive training game designed to help players identify and challenge negative thought patterns. Based on Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) principles, this game trains the brain to recognize, interrupt, and redirect harmful thought patterns while building mental resilience and emotional regulation skills.

## 🎮 How to Play

### Basic Controls
- **Click/Tap**: Click on negative words that appear on screen
- **Avoid Positive Words**: Don't click on positive or neutral words
- **Quick Recognition**: Identify negative thoughts as they appear
- **Mental Training**: Practice cognitive restructuring in real-time

### Game Mechanics
1. **Word Display**: Various words appear on screen in different categories
2. **Target Recognition**: Click only on negative words (targets)
3. **Avoid Distractions**: Don't click positive or neutral words (distractors)
4. **Speed Challenge**: Make decisions quickly as words appear
5. **Encouragement**: Receive positive feedback for correct identifications

### Word Categories
- **Negative Words** (Targets - Click These): failure, worthless, hopeless, stupid, ugly, useless, weak, pathetic, disaster, terrible, awful, horrible, loser, reject, burden, mistake, embarrassing, shameful, guilty
- **Positive Words** (Distractors - Don't Click): success, valuable, hopeful, smart, beautiful, useful, strong, amazing, wonderful, great, awesome, fantastic, lovely, winner, accepted, helpful, perfect, proud, confident, peaceful
- **Neutral Words** (Distractors - Don't Click): chair, table, book, phone, water, tree, car, house, computer, music, coffee, paper, window, door, clock, shoes, jacket, sandwich, keyboard, mirror

3. Run the game: `python play.py`
4. Open your browser to `http://localhost:8000`
